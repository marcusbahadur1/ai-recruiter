Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 April 2026  ·  Effective date: 12 April 2026

AIWorkerz Pty Ltd ("AIWorkerz", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of everyone who uses our platform. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights in relation to that data.

By using the AIWorkerz platform or website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

1. Who We Are

AIWorkerz Pty Ltd is an Australian company providing an AI-powered recruitment automation platform. We act as a data controller in respect of the personal data of platform users (recruiters, hiring managers, administrators), and as a data processor in respect of candidate personal data processed on behalf of our customers.

2. What Data We Collect

Account and User Data

  • Full name and work email address when you create an account
  • Company name and role/job title
  • Billing contact details where applicable
  • Login credentials (passwords are stored in hashed form and never readable by us)

Candidate Data (processed on behalf of customers)

  • Name, email address, and contact details submitted in resumes or sourced during talent discovery
  • Resume content including employment history, education, and skills
  • AI-generated scores, assessments, and competency test responses
  • Audio and video recordings where a candidate completes an audio or video competency test
  • Communications sent to or received from candidates via the platform

Usage and Technical Data

  • Log data including IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited
  • Feature usage patterns and session data used to improve the platform
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 9)

3. How We Use Your Data

To Provide the Platform

  • Create and manage your account and subscription
  • Process job postings and run automated recruitment pipelines
  • Score and rank candidates, generate competency tests, and send outreach emails
  • Deliver hiring manager notifications and interview invitations

To Improve the Platform

  • Analyse aggregated, anonymised usage patterns to improve features
  • Diagnose technical issues and monitor platform reliability
  • Develop and test new features and improvements

To Communicate with You

  • Send transactional emails such as account confirmations and billing receipts
  • Respond to support requests and enquiries submitted via the contact form
  • Share product updates and important platform notices (you may opt out of marketing messages at any time)

Legal and Compliance Purposes

  • Meet our legal obligations under applicable Australian and international laws
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and protect the rights and safety of users

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable data protection law requires a legal basis for processing, we rely on the following:

  • Contract performance — processing necessary to provide the services you have signed up for
  • Legitimate interests — improving the platform, fraud prevention, and platform security
  • Legal obligation — where we are required to process data by applicable law
  • Consent — for optional marketing communications and cookies where required

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Infrastructure and cloud providers — we use reputable cloud service providers to host, store, and process data securely
  • AI processing providers — candidate responses and resume content may be processed by AI systems to generate scores and assessments
  • Email delivery providers — we use secure email delivery services to send platform and outreach emails
  • Payment processors — billing data is handled by a PCI-compliant secure payment processing provider; we do not store full card details
  • Legal requirements — we may disclose data if required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights

All third-party providers are contractually required to handle personal data in accordance with applicable privacy law and our data processing agreements.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to provide the platform services and meet our legal obligations. Specific retention periods are as follows:

  • Account data — retained for the duration of your subscription and for up to 90 days following account closure, after which it is deleted or anonymised
  • Candidate data — retained in accordance with the retention policy set by the account operator; platform defaults are configurable
  • Audit logs — retained for up to 12 months for compliance and security purposes
  • Billing records — retained for 7 years as required by Australian tax law

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us via the contact form on our website. We will process deletion requests within 30 days, subject to any overriding legal retention requirements.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights in respect of your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request that inaccurate or incomplete data is corrected
  • Deletion — request erasure of your personal data (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Portability — request a machine-readable export of your data
  • Restriction — request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — withdraw consent for marketing or optional processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via the contact form on our website. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

8. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Measures we implement include:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS) and at rest
  • Access controls limiting staff access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security reviews and vulnerability assessments
  • Secure cloud storage with reputable, certified infrastructure providers

No system can guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach affecting your rights and freedoms, we will notify affected users and, where required, relevant authorities in accordance with applicable law.

9. Cookies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to operate correctly and to understand how visitors use our site. For full details, please see our Cookie Policy.

10. International Data Transfers

AIWorkerz is based in Australia. Some of our service providers may process data outside Australia, including in the European Economic Area and the United States. Where data is transferred internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as standard contractual clauses or equivalent mechanisms.

11. Children's Privacy

Our platform is intended for use by businesses and professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under the age of 18. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, we will delete it promptly.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to our practices or applicable law. We will notify you of material changes by posting a notice on our website or sending an email to the address associated with your account. The updated policy will take effect on the date indicated at the top of the page.

13. Contact for Privacy Enquiries

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests relating to this Privacy Policy or the way we handle your personal data, please contact us via the contact form on our website. We aim to respond to all privacy enquiries within 30 days.
