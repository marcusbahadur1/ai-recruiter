Two Powerful Modes

Built for every hiring scenario

Whether you're hunting passive candidates or managing a flood of applications, AIWorkerz has a mode for it.

🤖
AI Talent Scout
Proactively discovers passive candidates across professional networks, enriches their profiles with work history and skills, scores them against your requirements, and sends personalised outreach — all hands-free.
  • Candidate discovery via AI-powered search
  • Full profile enrichment (skills, experience, education)
  • AI scoring 1–10 against job requirements
  • Personalised outreach email per candidate
  • Contact discovery — emails found automatically
  • Automatic retries — never misses a candidate
📄
Resume Screener
Post your job and share an email address. Every resume that lands in your inbox is automatically parsed, AI-scored, and progressed through a competency test — with zero manual effort.
  • IMAP email polling — no manual checking
  • PDF, DOCX, DOC resume parsing
  • AI resume scoring with strengths & gaps
  • AI-generated competency test (5 questions)
  • Text, audio, or video answer format
  • Hiring manager notification with full scorecard
